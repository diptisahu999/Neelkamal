Odoo IndiaMart Connector

With Odoo IndiaMart Connector you can easily create leads from API responses. You will be able to check the history of every api call. Also you can configure indiamart for more than one company. You can fetch response with scheduler and also with specific time duration.

Integration of IndiaMart

Visit seller.indiamart.com, login to your account, then go to:
Settings > Account Settings available on the left navigation.
Now click on Generate Key. A unique CRM key will be received on your registered email-id.
